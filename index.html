Mercedes, Uruguay

Nestled between Brazil and Argentina, Uruguay is a vibrant, progressive country known for its stunning Atlantic coastline, rolling internal grasslands, and exceptionally high quality of life. Home to roughly 3.4 million people, it stands out in Latin America for its strong democratic institutions, advanced social laws, and commitment to green energy—generating nearly all of its electricity from renewable sources. Whether you are walking along the historic streets of Colonia del Sacramento, enjoying the bustling beach culture of Punta del Este, or sharing a traditional mate (an herbal tea) with locals in the capital city of Montevideo, Uruguay offers a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere that punches far above its weight on the global stage.
